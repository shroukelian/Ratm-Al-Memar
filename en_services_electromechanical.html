Electromechanical Works (MEP)

We ensure the efficient and reliable operation of your facility through the integrated execution of electrical, plumbing, and fire suppression systems, thanks to pre-coordination via BIM technology.

Electrical Works

We cover a wide range of power distribution and control works, committed to the Saudi Building Code and Saudi Electricity Company (SEC) standards.

  • Medium and Low Voltage installations
  • Installation of Main Distribution Boards and Control Panels (MDBs & SMDBs)
  • Energy-efficient indoor and outdoor lighting systems
  • Earthing and Lightning Protection Systems
  • Low Current Systems: Cameras, Data Networks, and Access Control Systems

Plumbing Work

Design and execution of water and sewage networks and fire protection systems according to the highest standards (NFPA and UL/FM).

  • Cold and hot water supply networks (PPR & CPVC)
  • Sewage and stormwater drainage systems
  • Fire pumps, piping, and sprinkler networks
  • Installation and extension of sanitary systems in bathrooms and kitchens
