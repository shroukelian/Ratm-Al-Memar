The Concept of the Integrated General Contractor

Ratm Al-Memar's services transcend the traditional contracting model. We manage the entire project, starting from the **Foundational Phase (excavation and concrete)** through **Structural Construction**, and culminating in **Final Operation and MEP Systems**. This comprehensiveness guarantees our clients a clash-free project, executed with maximum time and cost efficiency.

  • Special focus on office towers, commercial buildings, and large mall projects.
  • Specialized engineering team for coordinating Civil Works with Mechanical Systems (HVAC, Fire, Plumbing).

Human Expertise and Material Resources

Our success relies on engineers and technicians of diverse nationalities (especially Filipino and Indian), selected based on their track record in major regional projects. Furthermore, we operate our own facility for manufacturing air ducts, reducing supply chain complexity and ensuring quality compliant with international standards.

Maintenance Division: A Commitment to Continuity
