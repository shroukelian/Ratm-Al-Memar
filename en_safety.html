Plans Approval and Civil Defense Licenses

We undertake on behalf of the client the preparation and approval of safety plans in engineering offices accredited by the Civil Defense. This includes obtaining and renewing the Civil Defense license according to their requirements, ensuring the project proceeds with minimal bureaucratic obstacles.

  • Submitting documents and requirements to finalize safety site service procedures
  • Obtaining and renewing Civil Defense and Municipality licenses
