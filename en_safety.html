Safety and Civil Defense Systems

We are your approved partner to ensure the highest standards of safety and protection in commercial and office facilities, with full commitment to Civil Defense requirements.

Supply and Installation of Safety Equipment

We supply and install all equipment and items required by the Civil Defense, including alarm and extinguishing systems, smoke detectors, and structural protection methods in accordance with international and local standards.

  • Manual fire extinguishers (various types)
  • Basic and smart fire alarm devices
  • Emergency lighting and evacuation route systems
  • Fire Protection Coatings (Intumescent Paint) for steel structures

Plans Approval and Civil Defense Licenses

We undertake on behalf of the client the preparation and approval of safety plans in engineering offices accredited by the Civil Defense. This includes obtaining and renewing the Civil Defense license according to their requirements, ensuring the project proceeds with minimal bureaucratic obstacles.

  • Submission of necessary documents and requirements to finalize safety site service procedures
  • Obtaining and renewing Civil Defense and Municipality licenses

Advanced Extinguishing and Control Systems

We design and install integrated fire extinguishing systems (sprinklers, UL/FM pumps, piping networks), in addition to advanced early detection alarm systems, and smoke management systems to ensure protection and adequate time for evacuation.
