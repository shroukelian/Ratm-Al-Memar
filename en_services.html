Comprehensive General Contractor Services: From Foundation to Operation

We are a general contracting company based in Riyadh, specializing in integrated project execution. We are committed to the highest standards of quality and safety, seeking client satisfaction in every project.

1. Excavation & Structural Works

  • Excavation, Backfilling & Site Preparation
  • Execution of Foundations and Concrete Bases
  • Reinforced Concrete Works (Columns, Slabs, Beams)
  • Block & Masonry Works (Internal and External Walls)

2. Mechanical Works

  • Air Conditioning and Ventilation Systems (HVAC)
  • Plumbing Systems (Hot and Cold Water)
  • Drainage and Storm Water Systems
  • Fire Fighting and Water Supply Systems (Piping & Pumps)

3. Electrical Works

  • Low and Medium Voltage Wiring (Power Distribution)
  • Distribution Panels, Control Panels, and Lighting
  • Grounding and Lightning Protection Systems
  • UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) and BMS Systems

4. Safety and Civil Defense Systems

  • Supply and Installation of Certified Fire Alarm & Extinguishing Systems
  • Smoke Control and Staircase Pressurization Systems
  • Civil Defense Approval and Licensing

5. Finishing Works

  • Flooring and Wall Finishes (Tiles, Marble, Paints)
  • False Ceilings, Glass, and Door Works
  • Water and Thermal Insulation Works
  • All Internal and External Finishing Works

6. Maintenance Division: Operational Sustainability Contracts

Scope of Specialized Contracts

  • Comprehensive Safety and Civil Defense Systems Maintenance Contracts
  • Maintenance Contracts for HVAC, Ventilation, and Cooling Systems
  • Full Building Maintenance Contracts for Commercial and Residential Facilities
