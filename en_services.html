Integrated General Contractor Services: From Foundation to Operation

We are a general contracting company based in Riyadh, specializing in integrated project execution. We adhere to the highest quality and safety standards and strive for client satisfaction in every project.

1. Excavation & Structural Works

  • Excavation, backfilling, and site preparation
  • Execution of foundations and concrete bases
  • Reinforced concrete works (Columns, Slabs, Beams)
  • Blockwork and brickwork (Internal and External walls)

2. Electromechanical Works (MEP)

Integrated execution of mechanical and electrical systems (excluding HVAC) ensuring optimal coordination.

  • Low and Medium Voltage distribution
  • Distribution panels, control, and lighting
  • Plumbing, sewage, and stormwater drainage systems
  • Earthing and lightning protection systems

3. HVAC & Ventilation Works

Our main specialization is in HVAC for major buildings and commercial facilities. You can explore our scope in detail.

  • Central AC and Chillers
  • VRF / VRV Systems
  • Duct fabrication and installation
  • Ventilation and smoke extraction systems
View All HVAC Solutions

4. Safety & Civil Defense Systems

  • Supply and installation of approved fire alarm and fighting systems
  • Smoke control and staircase pressurization systems
  • Obtaining Civil Defense approval and licensing for the project

5. Finishing Works

  • Flooring and wall works (Tiles, Marble, Paint)
  • False ceilings, glass, and doors
  • Waterproofing and thermal insulation
  • All interior and exterior finishing works

  • HVAC & Refrigeration maintenance contracts
  • Comprehensive maintenance for buildings, commercial, and residential facilities
